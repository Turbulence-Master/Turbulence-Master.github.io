Living cost in Lille


A monthly living cost in Lille, including lodging, food and transportation is estimated at 600 €/month. Here are some details on some costs linked to your stay:

Health and insurance

Student health insurance Included in registration fee Mandatory for non European
Immigration office stamp 55€ Preliminary to staying permit
Mutual health insurance (mandatory) 112€/year Allows to be refunded 100% on health care
Civil responsability (mandatory) 29€/year In case you cause an accident

Lodging

Student appartement on Campus
Single appartment from 200€ to 400€/month depending on size (see FAQ for more details)
Private appartement
Small appartment in Lille downtown from 350€ to 500€ depending on size and location

Meal

University restaurant (self service) 150€ Per month based on two meals per day

Transportation

Metro - Bus 13€ for a booklet of 10 tickets
25€ Monthly card

Various

"Baguette" bread 1.00€
Sandwich 2.50€-5.00€ Depending on content
Glass of beer in pub 3.50€
Cinema seat 7€-12€
Return train trip to Paris 40€ Non refundable ticket
Restaurant meal 10€-30€ For basic to standard meals, good french cuisine will be more expensive.
